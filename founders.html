Founders

Sylvester Amponsah, Co-Founder & CEO

Sylvester, co-founder of Vestein Investment Group, is a Master’s of Electrical Engineering
candidate and a Gates Millennium Scholar at Case Western Reserve University. He is one of the ten winners of the 2015 OZY Media Genius Awards. Sylvester started trading stocks in 2012. Despite his five years of experience, he does not classify himself as an expert. His curiosity in new technology led him to research about Ethereum and other altcoins in December of 2016. He invested in Ethereum at $10 per share in January of 2017 and gained a 40x return on his initial investment. He has subsequently invested in numerous altcoins and ICO’s. These days he is more interested in using statistical models to study the cryptocurrency market.

Joshua Kumi, Co-Founder & CFO

Joshua Kumi, co-founder of Vestein Investment Group, is an AML Investigator at US Bank and has worked as an analyst for Nationwide Insurance. Joshua holds a Bachelor's of Arts in Economic and International Studies from Denison University. From 2011 to 2015, Joshua conducted various market research with the Denison Investment Club, which managed 100k worth of assets. Recently, Joshua invested in NEO around $9 per share and saw the price rise to $58 per share. His nearly 500% profit margin from NEO has assured him of the potential returns in the crypto space. He has since identified various market trends which has resulted in hefty returns. Presently, he is dedicated to understanding the complexities of the cryptocurrency market and its market trends to maximize profit for clients.

Amjed Osman, COO

Amjed Osman, Chief Operating Officer at Vestein Investment Group, is a Customer
Success Manager at Lingo Live where he manages client relationships across the technology sector. Amjed holds a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with a Specialization in Finance from The Ohio State University. Prior to joining Lingo Live, Amjed served as a financial services consultant at Ernst & Young serving hedge funds, investment and commercial banks, asset managers, and broker dealers across the market in their strategic and regulatory initiatives. His experience ranges from top of the house strategy work in Compliance through Performance Improvement projects to mitigate risk. Amjed is dedicated to simplifying the complex world of cryptocurrencies and leveraging the technology infrastructure to help clients earn a profit.
