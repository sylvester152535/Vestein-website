Press

Founded in December 2017, Vestein Investment Group is a pioneering hedge fund that combines in depth analysis of macro and micro economics with market trends to create profitable cryptocurrency portfolios for clients. We rely heavily on transparency as one of our core values in dealing with clients. We are aiming to transcend current microeconomics understanding of markets by applying statistical analysis to cryptocurrency trading. Our asset allocation decisions are based on convex optimization models and time series analysis. Vestein Investment Group has raised approximately 100 thousand dollars from investors.
