About US

Brief History

Vestein Investment Group was started in 2017 at the wake of the Ethereum boom. The founders began to see the potential of cryptocurrency after witnessing the successful run of Ethereum, Ark, Stratis, Litecoin, and many other altcoins. The exponential rate at which some of the cryptocurrencies grew after their Initial Coin Offering (ICO) served as evidence that cryptos were not to be taken lightly as an investment option. For instance the price per share of bitcoin surpassed the price of the S&P 500 in April of this year as shown figure 1. This incredible bitcoin milestone took less than a decade to materialize.

Furthermore, the SEC’s regulation interest in the crypto space offered more evidence that, perhaps, cryptocurrencies would be legitimized in the foreseeable future. Putting the global money in perspective as shown figure 2, it is clear that the market cap of bitcoin, which was approximately 41 billion in early 2017, is on an upward trajectory. This 41 billion market cap has more than doubled since the publication of figure 2. Moreover, the whole cryptocurrency market cap has increased more than 70 billion in the same time span. All these factors reinforced the founders’ vision that cryptocurrencies would make a lucrative long term investment. These conclusions led to the birth of Vestein Investment Group.
